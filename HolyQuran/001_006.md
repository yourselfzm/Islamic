# اُهْدِنَا اُلصِّرَاطَ اُلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

## Ihdina alssirata almustaqeema

## 翻译(Translation)：

| Translator | 译文(Translation)          |
| :--------: | -------------------------- |
|    马坚    | 求你引领我们正路，         |
|  YUSUFALI  | Show us the straight way,  |
| PICKTHALL  | Show us the straight path, |
|   SHAKIR   | Keep us on the right path. |
